Smart Drone & Road

Unifying Aerial and Ground Data — At the Heart of Intelligent Infrastructure Monitoring

CEN integrates smart drone technology with smart road infrastructure to provide an intelligent monitoring solution capable of managing roads, traffic, facilities, environment, and disaster response. By utilizing multi-source data processing (road + aerial), AI-based risk detection, and integrated platform capabilities, we lead the digital transformation of national infrastructure.

"From customized big data platform implementation to AI-driven analytics—transforming data into value."
