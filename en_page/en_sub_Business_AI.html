AI Education Division

Practical AI Training to Build Digital Competency

The CEN AI Education Division provides customized training based on generative AI technologies, enabling anyone to learn easily and apply skills directly to real-world work. From promotional video and film production to task automation, digital crime prevention, and SNS content strategies, we deliver the essential competencies needed in the digital transformation era in a structured way.

AI Education Division Curriculum

Promotional Video and Film Production Using AI

Objective
Enhance the ability to efficiently and creatively produce video content using generative AI tools
Target Audience
General public interested in video production, marketers, and content creators
Duration
Total of 5 weeks (2 sessions per week, 2 hours per session)
Method
Theory lectures + project-based hands-on training

Understanding and Using AI Video Production Tools

  • Introduction and hands-on practice with the latest AI video tools such as Midjourney, Runway Gen-3, SUNO, UDIO, and ElevenLabs

Prompt Engineering Techniques

  • How to write effective prompts and analyze various case studies

Video Planning and Storyboard Writing

  • Writing scenarios and organizing storyboards using ChatGPT

Hands-on Video Production Using AI

  • Practice the full production process: image generation, video editing, and voice synthesis

Final Presentation and Feedback

  • Present completed video content and receive feedback from instructors and peers

Voice Phishing and Digital Crime Prevention Training

Objective
Understand the types and techniques of digital crimes, and enhance prevention and response capabilities through real-life case studies.
Target Audience
Employees of public institutions, companies, educational organizations, and general citizens
Method
Theory lectures and hands-on workshops

Understanding Digital Crimes

  • Definitions and characteristics of voice phishing, smishing, pharming, etc.
  • Analysis of recent digital crime cases

Voice Phishing Prevention Strategies

  • Introduction to major tactics and damage types
  • How to respond in suspicious situations
  • Case studies involving impersonation of financial institutions and family members

Smishing and Pharming Response Methods

  • How to identify suspicious messages and links
  • Smartphone settings for personal data protection
  • Safe internet usage habits

Practical Exercises and Simulations

  • Mock training for responding to phishing calls
  • Analyzing suspicious messages and establishing response strategies
  • Practicing the reporting and response procedures in case of damage

Business Efficiency Training Using Generative AI

Objective
Learn how to enhance efficiency in various business tasks—such as document creation, data analysis, and workflow automation—using generative AI tools like ChatGPT.
Target Audience
Public institution and corporate employees, managers, and education coordinators
Method
Lecture-based and hands-on workshop

Overview and Use Cases of Generative AI

  • Understanding the concept and core features of generative AI
  • Examples of how AI improves business productivity

Automating Document Creation Using ChatGPT

  • Practice writing reports, proposals, emails, and more
  • Learn prompt engineering techniques
  • Summarize data and extract insights using ChatGPT
  • Basic data visualization practice

Automation and Integration with Work Tools

  • How to integrate ChatGPT with tools like Excel, Slack, etc.
  • Examples of automating business processes

Security and Ethical Considerations

  • Privacy protection and security concerns when using AI
  • Guidelines for ethical AI usage

SNS Utilization Training (Blog, YouTube, Instagram, etc.)

Objective
Effectively use various SNS platforms such as blogs, YouTube, and Instagram to enhance personal branding and marketing skills.
Target Audience
Self-employed individuals, marketers, content creators, general public
Method
Lecture-based and practical workshops

Understanding SNS Platforms and Strategy Building

  • Methods for developing effective content strategies

Using Blogs

  • Content planning and writing practice
