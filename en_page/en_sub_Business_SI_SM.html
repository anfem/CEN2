Reference Projects
Key Projects

Bioethics Institution Management System – KDCA

Objective
Centralized management of institutions and IRB deliberation records based on the Bioethics Act
Features
  • Role-based integrated operations across researchers, IRBs, and institutions
  • Full-cycle automation of IRB application, approval, and archiving
  • Real-time law application through national legislation API integration

Genetic Testing e-IRB System – KDCA

Highlights
  • Digitalization of IRB review procedures for genetic testing institutions
  • Management and tracking of human biological sample research records
  • Secure repository setup for IRB members and review documents

Blood Safety Management System – KDCA

Objective

Establishing a full-cycle blood safety management system from donation to adverse event monitoring

Features
  • Tracking of blood product history and adverse case reporting
  • Monitoring of blood supply status by region
  • API integration with KCDC and notification to regional centers

MTS: Traffic Enforcement Equipment Management – Road Traffic Authority

Features
  • Centralized simulator evaluation for senior drivers
  • System linkage of assessment records and aptitude test results
  • Supports decision-making for driver's license eligibility

Drone Integrated Management System – Korea Transportation Safety Authority

Objective

Establishing a shared On/Off integrated drone safety information system between TS, Seoul Aviation Office, and Korea Airports Corporation

Scope
  • Registration, maintenance, and flight plan management of unmanned aerial vehicles
  • User authentication, inspection logs, and automatic detection of no-fly zones
  • Platform designed in alignment with national drone policy
